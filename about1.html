India’s Ultimate Online dental Shopping Destination. Dentalkart’s vision is to create India’s most reliable and frictionless commerce ecosystem that creates life-changing experiences for buyers and sellers.

Our Story

In January 2014, Dr Vikas Aggarwal along with sandeep Aggarwal, started Dentalkart.com – India’s largest online Dental market, with the widest assortment of 35,000 plus products across 80 plus diverse categories from over 125 regional, national, and international brands. With Thousands of users.

Dentalkart is the shopping destination for Dentists across the country, delivering to 6000+ cities and towns in India. Online Shopping – A Boon The trend of online shopping is becoming a household name and so is Dentalkart.

What we offers

Shopping online particularly at Dentalkart is a child’s play; all you need is a mobile phone or laptop or tablet with Internet connection to get started. Simply log into Dentalkart.com and browse through the wide assortment of products across categories.

Once you have zeroed in on your favourite products, simply place the order by filling in the details; the products will be delivered right at your doorstep.
